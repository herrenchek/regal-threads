Meet Mary Theresa

Gentlemen's Clothier and Stylist

A magician of sorts, Mary Theresa Sciandra is President and Owner of Manhattan-based men’s private label custom clothing company Regal Threads.

“We’re changing the way men view their wardrobes and showing them how staying up-to-date can be an effortless process,” Mary Theresa says of Regal Threads.

Having worked in the clothing trade and women’s wear for twenty years, Mary Theresa always knew menswear was her calling. Having had a passion at a very young age for style, color and design, Sciandra chose studies and attended Misericordia University in Pennsylvania where she earned a Bachelor of Arts in Home Economics, specializing in Fashion Design and Merchandising.

Using her innate creative talents, with years of expertise and a keen eye to detail – she boasts an established reputation in client services. Regal Thread clients are accomplished, successful men who appreciate luxury and a professional service to simplify their busy lives.

Most men find shopping difficult and at the same time understand the importance of a well coordinated appearance. Mary Theresa makes sure that her clients have a wardrobe that meets their expectations professionally and personally. She applies her skills to fit the clients physical frame, prepares luxurious cloths of specific design, then styles the garments individually with undivided attention to detail, as for her – “it’s all about the client!”

Today, even after fifteen plus years in the business, her face lights up when talking about nothing better than a well dressed man and the satisfaction of her clients receiving many compliments.

“You will love my intentions to spoil you with luxury and personalized service for your brand-new wardrobe attire. I invite you to experience Regal Threads!”
